Welcome to my wiki page about films. Movies and web series are a fantastic source of entertainment, perfect for getting rid of boredom or passing some free time. I watch films on various occasions. Sometimes it's because a movie or series is genuinely good, other times it's due to the hype. Often, I watch films suggested by friends, family, or even random people. I might be drawn in by trailers, reels, shorts, or just browsing the internet. And yes, sometimes it's simply to pass the boredom.
This repository contains movies and web series that I've already watched or have on my watchlist. The main purpose of this list is to help me remember whether I've seen a film or not. It's a great way to keep track!

:::note
### For Other Users
If you're using this repository for reference, please note that not all movies listed here may be worth watching. However, one thing you can rely on is the IMDb rating. Trust me, I've never seen a good film with a bad rating or vice versa. So, before watching, just check the ratings, or feel free to watch anything that catches your interest.
:::

:::danger Disclaimer
> :warning: I haven't flagged any film for specific audiences, and it's not possible given the size of the list. So, watch at your own risk!
:::
